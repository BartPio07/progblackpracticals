German Cars Of This Century

Porsche

My favourite: Porsche GT3 RS

The porsche is an infamous car brand, specialising in sports cars. Creating some of the most popular and loved cars. Such as the Porsche 911, 718 Cayman, 718 Boxer. Over the years, Porsche has also started to include luxury SUV's into their range, such as the Cayanne and Macan. Porsche has competed in the Le Mans race (the worlds most prestigious endurance race) and has overall 19 victories.

Bmw

My favourite: BMW M3 Competition

BWM is also a very well known car brand, known for its performance, luxury and driving experience. The BMW name is an acronym for Bavarian Motor Works (in German, Bayerische Motoren Werke). BMW also produces many types of cars, from sporty coupes like the M4 and Z4, to comfortable sedans and SUV's like the 3 and 5 series and the X5. BMW also has their own racing brand of cars, more track focused, which are marked the an M competition, for example, M3 Competition.
